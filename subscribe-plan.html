WHY SHALL I BE ON DALEEL HUB

The Daleel Hub has a network of buyers all coming to find out what, how and where to buy particular goods and services.

They key benefits to you are:

1. Understand where the growth for your business is and how to improve your success rate

2. Show up in front of buyers at the right time with the right offering

WHO IS USING DALEEL HUB

The Daleel Hub is used around the world, by buyers & users from various oil companies, including super-majors, NOCs, IOCs, and EPC contractors large and small. Additionally, we support other companies from the financial services and the professional training sectors. We have growing network of 160 registered users and receive over 2,500 visitors to the site each month, who are mainly looking for 1) Suppliers and Contractors, 2) Prices & Trends 3) Latest technology success stories
